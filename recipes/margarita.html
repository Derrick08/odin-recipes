Margarita

Ingredients

  • kosher salt as needed
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 1 3/4 fluid ounces silver tequila
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 1/4 fluid ounces freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon agave syrup, or to taste

Directions

  1. Pour salt onto a plate. Rub a lime wedge around the rim of a glass, and dip the rim of the glass in the salt.
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add tequila, lime juice, orange juice, and agave syrup. Close the shaker and shake until completely chilled, about 30 seconds. Pour into the salted glass. Serve immediately.
